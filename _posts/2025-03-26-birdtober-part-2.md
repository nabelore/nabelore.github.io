<p>A confession: I kept going back to these birds, trying to rework a bit more color, a bit more texture into them, because they never felt quite right to my mind's eye. Don't know if I got them to a point I'm satisfied with, but they needed to be done because there are other birds to draw. Trying to finish more drawings and finish more sketchbooks. You can only learn so much by reworking the one sketch ad nauseum.</p>
