Hi, I'm Anabelle.
I'm an artist.

This website collects my adventures in:

  • drawing stories
  • painting light
  • sketching texture
  • ...and capturing my creative experiences with words

After graduating in 2012 with a degree in Multimedia Arts, I started taking on creative projects for different industries before focusing on comics and book illustration. I recently self-published a collection of journal comics from 2021, and am in the midst of working on Sun says Moon says, an original comic based on a Philippine folktale.

You can find most of my work on the internet posted under the moniker "anonabelle".

If you would like to work together, feel free to email at anonabelle (dot) art (at) gmail (dot) com.
