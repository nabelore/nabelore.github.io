Hi, I'm Anabelle.
I'm an artist.

This website collects my adventures in:

  • drawing stories
  • painting light
  • sketching texture
  • ...and capturing my creative experiences with words

Afer graduating with a degree in Multimedia Arts from DLS-CSB in 2012, I have tackled various projects for games, animation, comics and book illustration. I recently self-published a collection of journal comics created in 2021, and am in the midst of working on an original comic based on a Philippine folktale (more details in July 2022!).

You can find most of my work on the internet posted under the moniker "anonabelle".

If you would like to work together, feel free to email at anonabelle (dot) art (at) gmail (dot) com.
